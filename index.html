What is English Club ?

What is actually English Club? Well English Club is an Extracurricular in SMKN Bantarkalong with focus in developing students' English abilities, here we are given many interesting activities that can enhance our skill in English, such as speaking, writing, listening and reading.

Our activities during the meeting

So many interesting activities such as playing role play/drama, making video, reading and telling story, playing games, listening to English songs and joining English competition.

The reason why you must join English Club

Finnally why you must join English Club? Well guys, learning English with us is very fun. That's positive activity and it can increase our quality. We as teenagers we have to able to speak English helping us in our future. You know what? English is international language in the world. So, English is very important, and it's also a mother language for many countries.
